Chinese photographer makes partner disappear

Pixy Liao, the acclaimed Chinese photographer, remains in shock after the events occurred on Friday night in her flat located in the New York SoHo.

While cleaning her photography equipment, Ms Liao pushed her camera shutter accidentally, while pointing with it at her boyfriend. To her astonishment, the 45-year-old man disappeared behind the light of the flash and his whereabouts remain unknown.

Native Indian American's soul found inside roll film

The soul of Native Indian American Mangas Coloradas has been found inside a roll film of classic photographer Edward Sheriff Curtis.

The roll film has been located in an attic in Berlin's hipster neighbourhood Kreuzberg by new tenant Florian Schmidt inside a yellow plastic bag (Gelber Sack) that was meant to be deposited 70 years ago.

Is plastic surgery as we know it a matter of the past?

A group of Spanish computer scientists, together with a selection of all time Spanish photography stars, claim to have developed a method to perform cosmetic surgery making use exclusively of digital means.

A beta version of the revolutionary software could be available by the end of the year to the general public. While inventors are asking everyone to remain calm, the widespread rumours of the software having an affordable price, as well as including an undo function, has left the plastic surgery professionals trembling.
