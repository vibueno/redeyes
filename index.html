Chinese photographer makes boyfriend disappear

Pixy Liao, the acclaimed chinese photograper, remains in shock after the events occurred on Friday night in her flat in the New York SoHo.

While cleaning her photography equipment, Ms Liao pushed her camera shutter accidentally, while pointing with it at her boyfriend. To her astonishment, the 45-year-old man dissapeared behind the light of the flash and his whereabouts remain unknown.

Native Indian American's soul found inside roll film

The soul of Native Indian American Mangas Coloradas has been found inside a roll film of classic photographer Edward Sheriff Curtis.

The roll film itself has been found in an attic in Berlin's hipster neighbourhood Kreuzberg by current tenant Florian Schmidt inside a yellow plastic bag (Gelber Sack) meant to be deposited 70 years ago.

Is plastic surgery as we know it a matter of the past?

A group of Spanish computer scientists, together with a selection of all time Spanish photography stars, claim to have developed a method to perform cosmetic surgery making use exclusively of digital means.

A beta version of the revolutionary software could be available by the end of the year. While inventors are asking the general public to remain calm, the widespread rumours of an undo function included in the software has left the plastic surgery scene trembling.
